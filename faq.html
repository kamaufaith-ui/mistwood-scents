Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our products and orders.

Orders & Delivery

We deliver within Nairobi in 1-2 business days and to other towns across Kenya in 3-5 business days. Orders placed before 12pm are usually processed the same day.

Delivery within Nairobi costs KES 200. For other towns in Kenya, delivery costs KES 400-600 depending on location. Orders above KES 5,000 qualify for free delivery within Nairobi.

Yes! You can pick up your order from our shop on Ngong Road, Nairobi. We're open Monday to Saturday, 9am to 6pm. Just let us know when placing your order that you'd like to collect in person.

Not yet — we currently only deliver within Kenya. We're working on expanding to East Africa in the future. If you're outside Kenya and interested, feel free to contact us and we'll see what we can arrange.

Our Products

Our fragrances are original blends created under the Mistwood Scents brand. They are not copies or inspired versions of other brands — each scent has its own unique character and notes.

Most of our fragrances last 6-8 hours on the skin. Woody and oud-based scents (like Velvet Oud and Forest Rain) tend to last longer — up to 10 hours. Lighter fresh scents like Morning Mist typically last 4-6 hours.

The scent is exactly the same — only the bottle size and price differ. The 50ml option is great if you want to try a new scent without committing to a full bottle. The 100ml is better value if you already know you love it.

Most of our fragrances are suitable for everyday wear, but if you have very sensitive skin or known allergies, we recommend doing a patch test before full use. If you have specific concerns, feel free to contact us and we'll help you find the right scent.

Returns & Payments

Unfortunately we can't accept returns on opened perfumes for hygiene reasons. However, if your order arrives damaged or incorrect, we'll replace it at no extra cost. Please contact us within 48 hours of receiving your order.

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfer, and cash on delivery for Nairobi orders. We're working on adding card payments soon.

You can reach us through our Contact page, by emailing info@mistwoodscents.com, or by calling +254 712 345 600 Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm. We aim to respond to all enquiries within 24 hours.
