Our Story

How Mistwood Scents started.

Mistwood Scents started in 2021 as a small side project. We were tired of spending too much money on perfumes that smelled great in the shop but faded within an hour. We wanted something that actually lasted, smelled good, and didn't cost a fortune.

So we started sourcing our own fragrances — testing them, wearing them daily, and only keeping the ones we genuinely liked. What started as a personal hobby turned into a small business, and we've been growing slowly ever since.

We're based in Nairobi, Kenya and we deliver across the country. Every scent in our collection has been personally tested and approved by our team before it ever goes on sale.
