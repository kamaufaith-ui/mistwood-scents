Our Story

A small shop with a simple goal.

We started Mistwood Scents because we wanted a place to buy good perfume without the huge price tags. Our scents are picked to be easy to wear every day — at school, at work, or just hanging out — and we always keep a few lavender-based options alongside fresh and woody ones.

We deliver across Nairobi within 1-2 days, and to other towns in Kenya within 3-5 days. You can also pick up your order from our shop on Ngong Road.
